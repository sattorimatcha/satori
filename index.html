Sattori - My Matcha Story

I didn't discover matcha in some faraway Japanese temple—it happened in Pune.
A small café, a quiet moment, and a cup of something bright green that tasted… different. Smooth, earthy, calming. I didn't know then that it would become such a grounding force in my life.

Matcha felt like more than a drink. It was a ritual—something that helped me slow down, find focus, and feel clear without the chaos that coffee left behind.

But I struggled to find that same experience again. Most matcha brands felt too bitter, too clinical, or just didn't carry the soul of what I'd tasted. That's when the idea sparked:
What if I created a matcha brand that was rooted in mindfulness, but fit our fast-paced lives?
That honored tradition, but made space for flavor, creativity, and play.

And that's how Sattori was born.
Not just matcha—but a moment. A pause. A quiet kind of power.
